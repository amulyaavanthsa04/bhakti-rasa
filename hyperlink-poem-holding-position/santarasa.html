"Śānta" means peace, and "Śāntarasa" is the mood of loving devotion characterized by tranquility and serenity. It is the expression of love that is calm and composed, often depicted in the peaceful relationship between the devotee and the divine. Śāntarasa implies stability and a deep sense of inner peace in one's love towards the divine, it is unwavering because it is detached from worldly forms of love.
