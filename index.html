bhakti & rasa

This Harmonic Collection is a curation of poetry, literary art, music and the visual arts that explores the themes of devotion (bhakti) and essence (rasa).

The motivation of this collection was understanding how human beings experience faith and spirituality through various artistic expressions.

This website will be your guide, explanations and your portal to delve into the facets of faith through religious art, specifically from Hinduism and worship of a personified divinity.
